Services

  • Services
01

Service Law

Service Law forms the core strength of our practice. We regularly represent clients in matters involving:

  • Selection, Appointment, Promotion and Seniority Disputes
  • Disciplinary proceedings, Suspension and departmental enquiries
  • Pay fixation and service regularization
  • Pension and Retirement Benefits
  • Our sustained focus in this field has established PNK Legal LLP as a trusted authority in service jurisprudence.
02

Constitutional Law

Constitutional litigation is an integral part of our practice. We have successfully handled cases involving:

  • Enforcement of Fundamental Rights
  • Judicial Review of Administrative Actions
  • Challenges to Governmental and Statutory powers

Our commitment to constitutional principles and the rule of law remains steadfast and uncompromising.

03

Kerala Education Act & Rules

Kerala’s Education sector operates within a detailed statutory framework. Our firm has extensive experience in matters arising under the Kerala Education Act and Rules, representing Educational Institutions, Managements, Teachers, Non-Teaching Staff, Students and Stakeholders.

We provide strategic representation in service disputes, approval matters, regulatory compliance issues and statutory appeals.

04

University Matters

We possess substantial experience in handling disputes and regulatory issues involving Universities and affiliated institutions. Our expertise includes:

  • Approval and appointment disputes of Teaching and Non-Teaching Staff
  • Syndicate and Academic body decisions
  • Affiliation and Recognition issues
  • Service disputes within Universities
  • Proceedings before University Appellate Tribunals

We provide strategic and effective representation to Faculty Members, Managements and other Stakeholders in University-related litigation and advisory matters.

05

Panchayath and Municipality Matters

We advise and represent clients in matters relating to Local Self-Government Institutions, including Panchayaths and Municipalities. Our expertise covers:

  • Land use and zoning disputes
  • Building permits and licensing issues
  • Regulatory compliance
  • Administrative and statutory appeals

We ensure effective representation before Statutory Authorities and Courts.

06

Dispute Resolution & Arbitration

Dispute Resolution is a key pillar of our practice. We represent clients in Civil, Service, and Statutory Disputes before Courts, Tribunals and Quasi-Judicial authorities.

  • Invocation and conduct of arbitration proceedings
  • Appointment of Arbitrators
  • Representation in Domestic Arbitration matters
  • Interim relief proceedings
  • Enforcement and challenge of arbitral awards

We adopt a strategic and solution-oriented approach, ensuring effective representation both in litigation and Alternative Dispute Resolution Mechanisms

07

Paddy & Wetland Conversion Matters

We advise and represent clients in matters arising under the Kerala Conservation of Paddy Land and Wetland Act, including proceedings relating to land classification and conversion.

  • Filing and pursuing Form 5 applications before the Revenue Divisional Officer
  • Filing and pursuing Form 6 applications before the Local Level Monitoring Committee (LLMC)
  • Challenging adverse orders before appropriate statutory authorities and Courts
  • Advising on compliance and land utilization issues

We provide clear legal guidance to Landowners, Developers and Stakeholders navigating the regulatory framework governing paddy land and wetlands in Kerala.

08

Transactional Practice

Alongside our strong litigation practice, PNK Legal LLP maintains a robust transactional and advisory division. We understand that many legal matters require proactive structuring and preventive legal strategies beyond courtroom advocacy.

  • Drafting and negotiating contracts
  • Advising on statutory and regulatory compliance
  • Legal advisory in real estate transactions
  • Strategic counsel for businesses and individuals

PNK Legal LLP provides professional legal services with expertise in Service Law, Constitutional Law, Dispute Resolution, and Regulatory Compliance delivering reliable and effective legal solutions.
